Typography with food

Food, substance consisting essentially of protein, carbohydrate, fat, and other nutrients used in the body of an organisn to sustain growth and vital processes and to furnish energy.
The absorption and utilization of food by the body is fundamental to nutrition and is facilitated by digestion. /div>
Plants, which convert solar energy to food by photosynthesis, are the primary food source.

Animals that feed on plants often serve as sources of food for other animal.

Food is treated in a number of articales. For a description of the processes of absorption and utilizaion of food, see nutrition; nutrition, human; digestion; and digestive system, human.
